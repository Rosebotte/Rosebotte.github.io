Artless Timer

Artless Timer is an Android OS widget for giving you all needed information about time. Here are some futures in it.

  • Shows time in other time zone.

    You can choose from detailed or simple time zones list and get information about what time there at the moment.

  • Shows time of someone from your contact book, or company.

    You can choose to display the name, template or any text and choose their time zone, or adjust it manually.

  • Counter.

    Use counter to determine how much some process is last. For example the flight or the football game. You can use it as a cooking timer.

  • Countdown and periodic timer.

    Use countdown timer to check how many days, hours, minutes remains to some event. You can read/write the phone's Calendars. After event is occurred widget shows how long ago it was.

  • Adjustable appearance.

    You can change the color of the text or background, specify to show the time in 12 or 24 hour format and define whether display day of week or not.
